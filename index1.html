    Problem

    People spend long periods of time deciding which piece of furniture to buy and if it will fit their needs, budget, and style of their living space.

    Solution

    AR visualizing research tool that allows the user to customize their personal space.

    User Research

    objective
  • To understand how user plans for decorating a room. How they measure and do basic styling.
  • How might we help users have better experience, which is more efficient and less stressful when decorating their home.
  • To understand user's motivations to decorate their room.

    • 4 Interviews

    27 Surveys

    Summary

    Key Findings
  • people had a hard time visualizing furniture in the room.
  • they find inspiration in magazine, movies, books, Pinterest, etc.
  • people are spending months planning to buy new furniture
  • When furnishing the whole room people have hard time visualizing how all will fit together.
